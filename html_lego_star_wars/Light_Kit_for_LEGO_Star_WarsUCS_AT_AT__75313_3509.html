Details

This kit includes 3D Instructions for Light After Build.

Bring the Battle of Hoth to life with a cinematic Light My Bricks™ light kit for the LEGO® Star Wars™ UCS AT-AT™ 75313, crafted to highlight every iconic detail of this Imperial war machine. The head glows menacingly with red cockpit lights and firing blaster effects, while the cool white cockpit interior sets the stage for battle. Inside the main body, rotating micro lights and a mix of cool and red LEDs create immersive depth and motion, mimicking the chaos of onboard action. Beneath, Luke Skywalker dangles with a glowing blue lightsaber, poised to strike—making this light kit the ultimate enhancement for Star Wars fans and display collectors alike.

Light My Bricks™ Superior Componentry: Engineered for the Best Build Ever.

  • Ribbon thin flat cables designed with exact specifications for the LEGO System, to fit snuggly between studs, flat enough to thread between plates and around corners with no gaps.
  • Cable connectors have been designed specifically with reverse polarity so you can connect them easily and in any orientation, up or down.
  • All component casing and housing has been designed to connect seamlessly onto the LEGO stud system, with LEGO dimensional parts.
  • The most Powerful LED lighting, with epic effects specific to each build.

New to lighting your LEGO®?

Do I Have to Take My LEGO® Apart?

Not completely.

All our kits come with "light-after-you-build" 3D instructions that guide you through installing lights with only minimal disassembly, just a few bricks at a time, nothing major.

Some kits also include "light-as-you-build" options, letting you install as you go. Either way, the process is flexible, straightforward, and designed with beginners in mind.

Is It Safe for My LEGO® Sets and Long-Term Display?

Yes, completely. Our lighting system is specifically engineered for LEGO® and long-term use.

  • Low-Voltage Power: All kits operate on low voltage (typically 5V via USB), which is safe for both your LEGO® bricks and your home environment.

  • Smart Fuse Protection: Every component is equipped with built-in smart fuses to prevent overheating and power surges.

  • Heat-Free LEDs: Our lights run cool, meaning no risk of warping or damaging your bricks over time.

  • Enclosed Circuitry: Unlike other systems, ours features fully enclosed electronics—no exposed solder points, no mess, just peace of mind.

You can safely keep your lit-up set on display for hours or even days at a time. It's built for exactly that.

I’ve never used electronics. Can I still do it?

Yes, 100%. You don’t need any electronics experience to light up your LEGO® set with Light My Bricks. 

Our kits are designed for beginners. With plug-and-play components, clear part labelling, and guided 3D or video instructions. No wiring skills, no tools, and no tech knowledge required.

If you can build LEGO®, you can light LEGO® with Light My Bricks. We’ve made it that simple.

Why is Light My Bricks more expensive than competitors?

We call ourselves industry leaders for more than just being one of the first. We’ve spent years perfecting our lighting designs, components, and even packaging to create something that’s safe, easy to use, and kinder to the environment.

Our kits are not just lights. They are a complete upgrade to your LEGO® experience. That level of quality takes time, care, and top-tier materials. Yes, it comes at a cost, but we do everything we can to make our lighting as cost effective as possible without sacrificing what matters.

When you shop with Light My Bricks, you’re investing in the safest, classiest, and most environmentally conscious way to bring your LEGO® builds to life. We never settle and neither should you.

Are instructions included?

Yes. Every Light My Bricks kit comes with easy-to-follow instructions.

For light kits, you’ll get step-by-step 3D-style guides that feel just like LEGO® building instructions. For light bundles, we include clear video tutorials to walk you through everything.

Select light kits even let you choose how to light your set - during the build or after you’ve finished. And if you ever need help, everything is available right on the product page.

What if you don’t have a kit for my LEGO® set?

Our DIY components let you build your own custom setup. You can mix and match parts to fit almost any LEGO® set, even ones we don’t have kits for yet.

Want to know if a kit is coming soon? Check our Coming Soon page. Have a set you’d love us to light? Send us a suggestion. And if you need help building your own bundle, our support team is here to guide you.
